Our Learning Programmes

Building on Talent

Summary

Developing Young Managers with High Potentials

This course is designed to develop participants understanding of business in its broader context so that they can work more effectively across processes and functions in their organization. It will also unleash their leadership potentials.
Who Should Attend?
Middle level managers who have been identified with high potentials.
Benefits
At the end of this course participants will be able to:
  • Develop conceptual framework for analyzing businesses.
  • Gain better and practical understanding of organizational processes and systems with particular emphasis on those that cross functions
  • Challenge your assumptions about the business world and increase your understanding of business unit strategy
  • Discover your strengths and limitations
  • Develop your communication skills and ability to work in teams and lead them
  • Become a more effective member of your organization
Course Outline
  • Business context (broadening the business understanding)
  • Effective Strategy
  • Behavioural aspect (360 degrees assessment)
  • Organizational communications
  • Managing change
  • Problem solving
  • Measuring success
  • Teamwork

Corporate Social Responsibility

Summary

Stakeholder and Community Relation

Every organization has a link with the communities in which they operate. Within these communities exist a number of formal and informal groups that have interest in the operations of the organization.

This programme will help participants to understand the impact of their organization on the communities and how to ensure positive contribution as well as embed sustainability practices

Who Should Attend?
Managers who have the responsibility for planning and implementing engagements between the organization and the local communities in which they operate.
Benefits
  • Determine the CSR issues for your business in the marketplace, the environment and the community, as well as in the workplace
  • Identify and prioritize your organization's key stakeholders
  • Evaluate how responsible business practices can contribute to competitiveness and bottom line profitability
  • Define key performance indicators that would be relevant and useful to your company's stakeholders
  • Review your HR policies in light of growing demands for responsible business practice
Course Outline
  • What is corporate social responsibility and why is it relevant for businesses and other organizations?
  • The business case for operating responsibly: evidence from research
  • Stakeholders: prioritising them, understanding their needs, communicating with them
  • How corporate social responsibiity performance is intimately linked to company brands, reputation, and shareholder value
  • Corporate social responsibility in the workplace: the role of HR managers
  • Social impact: managing and measuring your social footprint
  • Practical guidance on how to target, measure and successfully report corporate social responsibility performance

Developing Emotional Intelligence Competencies

Summary
"Having academic intelligence hets you hired but lacking emotional intelligence gets you fired." This programme will provide individuals new knowledge and skills that will give them a deeper understanding of themselves.
Who Should Attend?
All senior and management staff
Benefits
At the end of this course participants will be able to:
  • Deal with challenges in a more constructive way in a given environment
  • Mentor and coach others in the organization
  • Develop good working and personal relationships
  • Recognize when your stress levels are out of control and return to a calm and energized state
  • Overcome relationship challenges without resorting to criticism as defensiveness
  • Read what others are feeling and communicate true needs
  • Understand self better
  • Work effectively unde pressure and increase bottom-line performance
Course Outline
  • Emotional Intelligence (EI) versus Intellectual Intelligence (IQ)
  • Working with emotional intelligence
  • Resilience and success
  • Relationships and communication
  • Mastering the five essential tools of emotional intelligence
  • Positive self perception assertiveness and present moment living (PML)
  • Cultivate a sense of purpose and meaning
  • Comprehensive work on stress management

HSE Awareness and Culture

Summary
HSE awareness and culture is essential for the success of every business activity. This learning event will assist participants to constantly make efforts to translate HSE awareness into daily practice. In other words to move from HSE on paper to HSE in the hearts and create the mind set for quality in the working environment.
Who Should Attend?
All personnel
Benefits
At the end of this learning event, participants will be able to:
  • Treat HSE as an integral component of all business activities
  • Guarantee quality in the work environment
  • Manage sincerely workplace HSE risks
  • Appreciate basic HSE know-how as frontline strategy for successful operations
  • Comply with HSE rules, regulations, standards and procedure
  • Appreciate the basic concept of corporate social responsibility (CSR)
  • Manage occupational health for the success of the business
  • Develop and support HSE competence
  • Manage efficiently transportation safety
  • Handle security and community issues
Course Outline
  • Introduction: HSE Focus
  • Management leadership in HSE
  • HSE-MS/ISO 14001
  • HSE rules, regulations and standards
  • HSE competence
  • Occupational practice and management
  • Safety and accident prevention/loss control
  • Environmental management
  • Transportation safety
  • Security management
  • Community issues
  • Expanded short term HSE improvement plan

HR Best Practices

Summary
People are the life blood of any organisation and they are pivotal to the achievement of business goals. This highly intensive and practical programme will provide delegates with proven strategies and techniques on how to become world class HR professionals and managers
Who Will Attend?
HR professionals and managers who wish to make a strategic impact on their organisation's success.
Benefits
At the end of this programme participants will:
  • Understand the key trends & developments in HR practice
  • Become an anchor for business transformation and success
  • Develop winning strategies on how to win the war for talents
  • Understand the magic of 21st century HR leaders
  • Understand how HR can influence and shape the future of the organisation
  • Develop strategies for creating future leaders in the business
  • Understand how HR leaders are turning adversities into opportunities
  • Understand how to improve professional ethics across the organisation
  • Identify ways to manage your HR processes more effectively and efficiently from both cost and resources perspective
  • Identify how competitors and industry leaders are handling employee communications, recruitment and retention and other related HR issues
  • Help HR become strategic partner in the organisation
Course Outline
  • Keeping abreast of the changes and evolution in HR practice
  • The requiremennt for a thriving 21st century organisation
  • Qualities of leading international HR managers
  • Developing winning HR strategies and policies
  • Knowledge sharing
  • How to develop effective reward strategies
  • How to get the best out of people
  • How to manage performance effectively
  • 360 degrees performance management system
  • Evaluation system that links individual performance to corporate business goals
  • How to forecast, attract and retain the best talents
  • Highlight performers
  • Compensation (Reward Ceremonies)
  • Authenticity and ethics for managers
