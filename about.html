About us

At Litesol, we are extremely focused and passionate about our vision. We are passionately committed and above all, we hold our customers in high esteem.

To ensure our high level commitment is never compromised, we make sure we target a client base we can effectively manage at any point in time; focusing more on internal expansion of the quality and volume of business we do with current clients rather than a rabid acquisition of more clients.

We are experts at generating high quality solutions that benchmark international standards globally. We anticipate future business challenges and scenarios which will guarantee you an edge over industry competitors

OUR VISION

To be the reference point for excellence in capacity development in Africa

OUR MISSION

We provide cutting-edge consultancy and Human Resources services to a highly discerning client-base

Our COMPANY CREDO

Right solutions come from the right people, processes and systems
