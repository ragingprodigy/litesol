HR Strategy and Renewed Thinking

Objectives


  • Construct strategies that establish the appropriate balance for Administrative and Strategic HR.
  • Explore the elements of transformational thinking through case studies
  • Examine the internal and external challenges to transfromational thinking and practices in corporations and suggest strategies to overcome them
  • COnnect transformational thinking to the design of Strategic HR Measurements. Develop the components of a draft HR Plan that aligns HR Strategic Thinking with Organizational Goals and Strategies
  • Review crucial elements of existing HR Information System and propose modifications
  • Enhance capaciity to build and sustain effective HR Teams.
  • Focus on materials and resources for further study


TOPICS TO BE EXPLORED


  • Balancing Administrative and Strategic HR
  • HR Team Building: Giving and Receiving Effective Feedback for Capacity Building in HR Administrative and Strategic Management
  • Crafting Strategic Objectives and Measurements (vs. Administrative Objectives and Measurements) to Achieve the Mission
  • Overview of HR Information System for Strategic HR Crisi Management with a Focus on Capacity Building in Human Resources
  • Principles and Steps for Successful Implementation of a Strategic Plan
  • Transformin gLeadership and Organizational Culture for Achieving the Mission

Dr. ASKIA DAVIS' Profile


Dr. Askia Davis who obtained his Masters & Ph.D degrees in Education specializing in Leadership & Organizational Development from COlumbia University, New York, is a writer, publisher and consultant to schools, universities, businesses and non-governmental organizations.
Askia specializes in Strategic Planning, Organizational Transformation, Team Building and enhancing educational and business outcomes

His 36 years work experience includes 17 years experience in the New York City Public School System where he worked as superintendent and senior assistant to 3 successive Chancellors to the New York Public School System. He has over 10 years experience with the New York Urban Coalition where he worked as a facilitator & strategic planner before he joined the New York Public School System.

His company Askia Book Publishing & COnsulting COmpany, has an expert team of partners who are renowned for capacity building in organizations that are undergoing organizational transformation.
