PRACTICAL APPLICATION OF LEADERSHIP COMPETENCY WORKSHOP

8am - 5pm, 24th - 26th March, 2015
Best Western Hotel, No 12 Allen Avenue, Ikeja, Lagos State. Download Time Table in PDF

Cost: N150,000 Per Participant

+ Expand all


COURSE OBJECTIVES

  • Identify planning as needful for managing work and others
  • Demonstrate the use of strategic management to effectively lead.
  • Be able to set goals and articulate a vision
  • Develop personal awareness and stress management skills
  • Understand the causes of conflict
  • Acquire the skills necessary to manage/resolve conflicts
  • Understand the nature of change and how to manage it
  • Demonstrate the characteristics of a team
  • Acquire the skills for developing future leaders
  • Be able to apply critical or analytical and creative thinking skills to solve problems
  • Understand NNPC’s position in the Oil & Gas Industry
  • Demonstrate the ability to write a good report


COURSE CONTENT

Programme for DAY 1
NNPC Culture & Values
  • Vision, Mission and Values Culture and Value of a Sustainable Organization
  • Desired Behaviours - (Integrity & Commitment)
Setting Goals and Articulating a Vision - Leadership vs Managerial Mindset
  • Leadership Without Management
  • Management Without Leadership
  • Symbolic Leadership
Managerial Planning
  • Planning as a Managerial Function
  • Developing Plans for Managerial Use
  • The Modern Approaches to Managerial Planning
  • Strategic Management
Syndicate Group Discussion/Presentations

Programme for DAY 2
EQ Competencies
  • The Discovery & Components of EQ
  • Mastering the 5 Essential Tools of EQ
  • Working with Emotional Intelligence
  • Stress Management
  • Positive Self-Perception
Managing Conflict
  • Types and Causes of Conflict
  • Dealing with Conflict
  • Conflict Management/Resolution
Change & Relationship Management
  • Sources & Forces of Change
  • The Nature of Change
  • Change Mgt. at Various Phases of the Change
  • Applying the Tools of Change Management
Building Effective Teams and Teamwork
  • Building Cross Function Team & Managers
  • Learning Organization
  • Characteristics of Work Group/Team
Developing Leaders Around You
  • Coaching, Counselling and Supportive Communication
  • Empowering & Delegation
  • Motivation & Reward
  • Discipline
Syndicate Group Discussion/Presentations

Programme for DAY 3
Analytical, Decision and Problem Solving Skills
  • Definition of Problem Analysis
  • Decision Making
  • Stages in Problem Analysis and Decision Making
  • Problem Analysis
  • Types of Decision Making Process
  • Individual vs. Group Problem Decision Making
Critical/Creative Thinking - Commercial Entrepreneurial Orientation
  • NNPC Goals and Policies
  • Basic Principles of Business Transactions
NNPC Position in the Oil & Gas Industry
  • Business Ventures
  • Value Chain
  • Upstream and Joint Venture Business
Downstream Sector of the Oil & Gas Industry
  • Refinery/Petrochemical Plants, PPMC Services
Business Communication & Report Writing
  • Various Types of Business Communication & Report
  • Recognize the Importance of Well-Produced Report
  • Actions & Features which Lead to Good Business Communication & Report
Syndicate Group Discussion/Presentations
